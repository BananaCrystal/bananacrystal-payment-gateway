The BananaCrystal Payments plugin allows you to accept payments for your store or business almost free on your woocommerce WordPress store easily.

== Description ==

#### WHY CHOOSE BananaCrystal? ####

Fast secure, low-cost, borderless, local and international payments in USD powered by blockchain/crypto payment rails.
Send and receive secure peer to peer payments to anyone instantly at no cost to you.
Privacy on all your purchases. Your bank or credit card company does not need to know what you are purchasingâ€¦because your purchases are private.

#### FEATURES ####
* Quick installation and setup.
* Easily take payment for a service from your site via BananaCrystal.
* Sell files, digital goods or downloads using your Stripe merchant account.
* Sell music, video, ebook, PDF or any other digital media files.
* Quick configuration with you BananaCrystal Payment Gateway.
